About Us

AB Shoes was founded in 2006 by Abdullah with a vision to make a unique mark in the shoe industry.
AB stands for individuality, creativity and self-expression. The chunky, sculptural designs of AB shoes make a bold styling statement. Wearing AB's expresses your confidence to be different and go against the expected norms.
Go-to’s that make you feel supported and happy and like “Ooh, I got this.” Some of the cushiest, comfiest, airiest, featheriest, lightest shoes on Earth — wait, make that the lightest shoes off Earth. Shoes so light, a butterfly could steal them. So soft, kittens seethe with jealousy. Shoes that are just like all the go-to’s you already know and love.
Leonardo da Vinci described the human foot as "a masterpiece of engineering and a work of art". So, to look after your feet better than anyone else, we study them – scrutinizing their biomechanics and movement at work, rest, and play to make sure they stay healthy and happy. Now, as always, our shoes are crafted using a hand-carved last – and we utilize both time-honored and pioneering construction techniques, technologies and materials to make sure Clarks are the shoes you can count on.
